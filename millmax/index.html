Private & White label

These business models are fundamentally different in terms of who determines the content of the product. We work on each of them and will help you choose the right one.
Private
label

Produced strictly according to the customer's specifications. All rights to the sale of goods belong to the customer.

  • exclusivity and fewer competitors
  • wide possibilities for customization
White
label

The manufacturer determines the composition, picking and packaging, but agrees with the customer on the branding of the finished product and has the right to sell it under their own brand or through other companies.

  • savings on investment during development and registration
  • faster and easier time to market

Documents

Paperwork and registration procedures are different for food products, cosmetics and dietary supplements. We take this into account when working under the laws of the EU and other countries. Our experts will tell you how to bring the product to the market legally and at minimal cost.
We will test every product, packaging and labeling. We will receive confirmation from independent experts that the product meets regulatory requirements. We will prepare a complete set of supporting documentation and translate it into any language. We will support you with applications, certification, declaration and payment of fees as well as after registration in case of disputes, notifications, product or label changes.
